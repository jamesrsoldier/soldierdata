Everything You Need to Win AI Search

From real-time monitoring to competitor benchmarking, Rangefinder provides the complete GEO analytics stack.

MON

Real-Time Visibility Monitoring

Automated daily scans query each AI engine with your tracked keywords and measure whether your brand appears in the generated response. Know exactly where you stand, every day.

CIT

Citation Extraction & Classification

Our extraction pipeline identifies every URL cited in AI responses, then classifies each as a brand citation, competitor citation, or neutral source. Four-stage detection covers direct links, brand names, domain mentions, and fuzzy matches.

SOV

Share of Voice Analysis

Measure your brand's citation share relative to competitors across each AI engine and keyword category. Track share of voice trends over time and identify where competitors are gaining ground.

SCR

Visibility Scoring

Position-weighted scoring algorithms calculate your visibility across engines. First-position citations score highest, with engine-specific weighting that reflects where your audience actually searches.

CMP

Competitor Benchmarking

Track up to 10 competitors per project. See which competitors get cited for your keywords, where they outrank you, and how their visibility trends compare to yours over time.

ALR

Smart Alerts

Configure alerts for visibility drops, new citations gained, lost citations, competitor changes, and negative sentiment detection. Get notified via email or in-app before small changes become big problems.

KWD

Keyword Management

Organize tracked keywords by category, toggle monitoring on and off, and analyze performance at the keyword level. See which queries consistently surface your brand and which need optimization.

GA4

GA4 & Search Console Integration

Connect Google Analytics 4 and Search Console to correlate AI visibility with actual traffic and search performance. Understand how AI citations translate into real visits and conversions.

The Monitoring Pipeline

How Rangefinder Works

An automated four-stage pipeline that runs daily to keep your visibility data current.

01

Keyword Scan

Each morning, Rangefinder queries all five AI engines with your tracked keywords — exactly as a real user would search.

02

Citation Extraction

Raw AI responses are parsed to extract every cited URL and brand mention using a multi-stage detection pipeline.

03

Scoring & Classification

Citations are classified as brand, competitor, or neutral. Visibility scores, prominence, and share of voice are calculated.

04

Alerts & Reporting

Alert conditions are evaluated against your thresholds. Dashboard metrics update. Trends become visible.

Analytics Framework

Metrics That Matter for GEO

Rangefinder calculates four core metrics that give you a complete picture of your AI search presence.

VS
Visibility Score
Weighted average across all engines. Google AI Overview carries 1.5x weight to reflect search volume.
CC
Citation Confidence
Percentage of query runs that contain at least one brand citation. Measures consistency of presence.
PS
Prominence Score
Position-based scoring. First citation = 1.0, second = 0.8, third = 0.6. Higher is better.
SoV
Share of Voice
Your brand citations divided by total citations, expressed as a percentage. Benchmark against competitors.
