Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most from prospective clients.

What industries do you serve?

We work across government, tribal organizations, finance, healthcare, manufacturing, and professional services. Our solutions are industry-agnostic by design but configured to meet sector-specific compliance and operational requirements.

How does generative engine optimization differ from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO optimizes for link-based search rankings. GEO focuses on how AI models like ChatGPT and Perplexity reference and cite your brand in their generated responses. It requires different content structures, authority signals, and monitoring tooling.

Can you work with our existing Dynamics 365 environment?

Absolutely. We regularly step into existing D365 deployments to troubleshoot performance issues, build custom modules, migrate data from legacy systems, and optimize configurations that were rushed during initial implementation.

What size projects do you take on?

From single-database optimization engagements to multi-year enterprise platform builds. We scope every project individually and provide transparent estimates based on actual requirements, not billable-hour targets.

When will the SaaS products launch?

SOPForge, CRMClean, and ProposalEngine are currently in development with early access programs opening soon. Contact us to join the waitlist and get priority access when each platform goes live.

Do you provide ongoing support after deployment?

Yes. Every project includes a post-launch support window, and we offer long-term managed services agreements for organizations that want continuous monitoring, optimization, and feature development.
